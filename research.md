* **Positioning Statement:** "The world's first medical institution dedicated solely to advanced Interventional Radiology and Vascular Medicine. We operate inside blood vessels and organs using micro-catheters, bypassing the need for standard surgical incisions, general anesthesia, and prolonged hospital stays."

## 2. Visual Style Guide & Design Tokens

To communicate elite medical authority and modern cutting-edge tech, Rythamo Hospitals uses a futuristic, high-contrast dark-mode theme. This design is highly clean, trust-inspiring, and premium.

### Typography:
* **Headings & Accents:** `Outfit` (Google Font) – Modern, clean geometric lines with an authoritative yet warm feel.
* **Body Text:** `Inter` (Google Font) – The industry standard for high-performance screen readability.

1. **Glassmorphism (`.glass-panel`):**

2. **Radial Ambient Glows (`.glow-bg`):**

## 4. Key Specialized Procedures Performed at Rythamo

As a boutique hospital specializing strictly in IR, our clinicians are world-class experts in these advanced image-guided operations:

### Vascular & Arterial Therapeutics
* **Angiography & IVUS:** Advanced real-time rendering of blood vessel pathways using intravascular ultrasound.
* **Angioplasty & Stenting:** Restoring complete blood perfusion to narrowed arteries by ballooning and placing cobalt-chromium alloy stents.
* **Thrombolysis:** Targeted micro-infusion of clot-dissolving agents (tPA) directly into acute deep vein thrombosis or arterial blockages.
* **Foreign Body Extraction:** Micro-catheter vascular recovery systems to safely retrieve migrated foreign bodies.

### Advanced Embolization
* **Uterine Artery Embolization (UAE):** A uterus-sparing, highly effective alternative to hysterectomy for uterine fibroids.
* **Prostate Artery Embolization (PAE):** Bypassing surgery to treat benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH).
* **Oncological Chemoembolization (TACE):** Direct tumor-starving chemotherapy combined with vessel-blocking micro-particles.

### Non-Vascular & Structural Guidance
* **Vertebroplasty & Kyphoplasty:** Injecting bone cement into collapsed vertebrae under dual CT/Fluoroscopy guidance to instantly relieve back pain.
* **Ultra-Precision Needle Biopsies:** Core tissue extraction under absolute target lock-on (breast, lung, liver, kidney) avoiding surgical incision.
* **Catheter Insertions (Dialysis & Chemotherapy):** Specialized long-term central access ports and hemodialysis catheter placement.
* **G-Tubes & Enteric Access:** Directly placing gastrostomy/feeding tubes through the abdomen under fluoroscopy.
