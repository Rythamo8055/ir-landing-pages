# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
* **Primary Patients:** Individuals seeking advanced, minimally invasive, non-surgical treatment options for complex conditions (such as uterine fibroids, severe leg blockages, acute clots, or spinal compression fractures). They are often anxious, in pain, and looking for reassurance and absolute clinical competence.
* **Referring Physicians:** Oncologists, gynecologists, cardiologists, and primary care physicians looking to send patients to a world-class, board-certified interventional radiology specialist center. They demand precise metrics, board-certified expertise, and clear clinical pathways.

## Product Purpose
To establish Rythamo Hospitals as the premier, highly specialized authority in Interventional Radiology (IR). The site must visually demonstrate the paradigm shift from invasive open surgery to precision, pinhole image-guided therapies, converting visitors into scheduled clinical consultations.

## Brand Personality
* **Organic Reassurance:** Warm, biological, expert, and deeply human. Bypasses the cold, computer-like, neon tech-navy clichés.
* **Atmosphere Adjectives:** Authoritative, comforting, cutting-edge, organic, human-centric.
* **Emotional Goals:** Instill a sense of absolute trust, safety, rapid relief, and modern medical excellence.

## Anti-references
* Sterile, generic clinical layouts (neon blue-and-white grids with cheesy stock images of smiling doctors).
* Overstimulating SaaS-dashboard aesthetics (neon icons on pitch-black background with hyperactive borders and glowing card outlines).
* Cluttered, multi-column portals that make anxious patients feel overwhelmed.

## Design Principles
1. **Pinhole Focus & Clarity:** Group complex data logically, and let breathing room dictate hierarchy.
2. **Organic Depth over Tech Slop:** Utilize warm, deep organic slate-teals and soft terracotta-coral accents to represent natural bodily blood vessels and tissue pathways.
3. **Clinical Trust through Transparency:** Present audited statistics and board certifications clearly with semantic and accessible structures.
4. **Reassuring Accessibility:** Keep line lengths readable (~65ch) and interactive focus borders highly visible for coarse or anxious inputs.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Strictly compliant with **WCAG 2.1 AA** color contrast and focus visibility rules.
* Dynamic scaling with responsive rem-based viewport clamp values.
* Focus management optimized for keyboard navigability and clear, coarse touch targets for patients with tremors or restricted mobility.
